Products

Bouquets

A detachable bouquet of flowers glued to polystyrene that can be bought with or without a green ground vase.

Saddles

An array of flowers glued and wired to a metal frame that can be fit over a headstone.

Memorials

A cut polystyrene shape that is decorated with flowers. They can be used on easel stands, hung on the wall, or attached to a cemetery vase.

Process

Bouquets

Each flower is glued on polystyrene. The bouquets are detachable so can be moved to another vase.

Saddles

Sealed polystyrene is glued and wired to the saddle frame before flowers are added.

Memorials

Polystyrene is cut with hotwire. The designer then paints, and seals the base. Flowers are added at the end.
